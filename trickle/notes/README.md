# Golden Bites Food Website

A responsive food website featuring two premium products with a yellow and matte gold theme.

## Features

- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
- Burger menu navigation for mobile devices
- Yellow/gold color theme with round bold fonts
- Logo and business name in header
- Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
- Multi-page structure (Home, About, Products, Contact)
- Two featured products with images and pricing

## Pages

- **index.html**: Homepage with hero section and products showcase
- **about.html**: About page with company information

## Components

- **Header**: Navigation bar with logo and collapsible menu
- **Hero**: Main banner section
- **Products**: Product showcase cards
- **Footer**: Footer with social links and copyright

## Theme Colors

- Primary: #F59E0B (Amber/Yellow)
- Secondary: #D97706 (Matte Gold)
- Accent: #FCD34D (Light Yellow)
- Background: #FFFBEB (Cream)
- Text: #78350F (Dark Brown)
